About FireGuard AI

FireGuard AI is an intelligent fire safety and monitoring system designed to detect potential fire hazards before they become dangerous. By combining real-time sensor monitoring with artificial intelligence, the system continuously analyzes temperature, smoke levels, gas leakage, and electrical current to identify risks and provide early warnings.

Our goal is to improve safety in homes, laboratories, industries, and public spaces by enabling proactive fire prevention rather than reactive response.

Our Mission

To make fire safety smarter, faster, and more accessible using modern technology and artificial intelligence.

Key Features

  • 🔥 Real-Time Fire Risk Detection
  • 🌡 Temperature Monitoring
  • 💨 Smoke and Gas Detection
  • ⚡ Electrical Current Monitoring
  • 📊 Live Dashboard Analytics
  • 🚨 Instant Alerts & Notifications
  • 🤖 AI-Based Risk Assessment
